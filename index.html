// ABOUT

Incubating the Future

Platinum Venture Labs was created to incubate and accelerate breakthrough ideas from the next generation of entrepreneurs. We identify, support, and invest in high-potential ventures led by exceptional early-stage founders.

Backed by the Poyiadjis Family Trust and extending the two-decade legacy of Platinum Capital Partners, PVL provides more than just capital. We embed ourselves alongside founders—providing strategic direction, operational expertise, and direct introductions to the family offices, institutional investors, and industry leaders who co-invest with us. Our model is built on conviction: fewer bets, deeper partnerships, and a long-term commitment to building companies that matter.

Strategic Guidance

Hands-on mentorship and strategic direction from seasoned investors with 20+ years of cross-sector experience.

Growth Capital

Flexible capital deployment tailored to each venture's stage and trajectory, from seed to scale.

Network Access

Direct access to family offices, institutional investors, and industry leaders across technology, energy, and defense.

Our Approach

01

Identify

We source exceptional founders working on frontier problems in AI, cybersecurity, and autonomous systems—filtering for technical depth, market timing, and founder conviction.

02

Invest

We deploy flexible, founder-friendly capital structured to match the venture's stage. Our deals range from pre-seed to growth equity, often co-investing with institutional partners.

03

Accelerate

We go beyond the term sheet. Our team embeds alongside founders to provide operational playbooks, technical advisory, and direct introductions to customers and partners.

04

Scale

We leverage the Platinum Capital Partners network—spanning family offices, sovereign funds, and global operators—to help portfolio companies reach international scale.

// OUR HERITAGE

Built on Two Decades of Proven Investment

Platinum Venture Labs is a subsidiary of Platinum Capital Partners Inc. (PCP), a holding company for the Poyiadjis Family Trust founded in 2005 by Roys Poyiadjis. Over the last 20 years, PCP has completed approximately 65 investment transactions across technology, energy infrastructure, real estate, private equity, and special situations—co-investing alongside family offices, institutional investors, and global industry leaders.

PVL extends this legacy into frontier technology, channeling two decades of cross-sector deal-making experience into backing the next generation of founders.

AI & Surveillance

Airship AI Holdings NASDAQ: AISP

Provided bridge financing for this AI-driven video and sensor surveillance platform. Airship went public on NASDAQ via reverse merger in Dec 2023. Platinum converted its senior secured convertible debenture to equity and fully exited by Dec 2024.

Energy Storage & Deep Tech

Nucleus Scientific / Indigo Technologies Co-founded with MIT

Platinum co-founded this Boston-based energy storage startup in 2007 with MIT Professor Ian Hunter. Assembled prominent VC co-investors. In 2015, Peter Thiel's $3B Founders Fund and Mark Heising acquired Platinum's stake.

Neuroscience & MedTech

NYU Neuroscience Ventures NYU Dept. of Neuroscience & Physiology

Collaborated with NYU to spin off patents into startups commercializing novel molecules, neuroscience devices, and technologies to suppress thalamocortical dysrhythmia under the leadership of Professor Rodolfo Llinás. Founder Roys Poyiadjis was inducted into the NYU Sir Harold Acton Society.

Energy Infrastructure

Kashima Biofuel Power Plant Kashima Port, Japan

Largest biofuel power plant in Japan. Joint venture with the family office of Martua Sitorus, CEO of Wilmar International (world's largest palm oil producer). Feed-in tariff valued at $1.5 billion over 20 years, launched after Japan's post-Fukushima shift away from nuclear energy.

Software & Behavioral Analytics

Cataphora Inc. Patented Technology

Patented software that models human behavior and interactions within massive data volumes. Platinum took a controlling stake, restructured operations, and sold the Legal Analytics division to Ernst & Young's Fraud Investigation & Dispute Services (FIDS).

Energy Infrastructure

Puerto Rico Natural Gas Pipeline Decentricity LLC

Exclusive franchise granted by the Puerto Rico government to operate a natural gas pipeline connecting all major PREPA power plants. Project capital costs expected at $600M with projected annual fuel savings of $400M by switching from diesel to natural gas.

LNG & Energy

Cyprus LNG Terminal $2.2B Capital Project

Formed a consortium with LNG Ltd (Australia) and SK E&C (Korea) to develop a 4 million ton LNG import terminal on Cyprus. Gas procurement from Cyprus' Block 12 field (5 Tcf reserves) owned by the Republic of Cyprus and Noble Energy.

Maritime & Shipping

Westport Tankers SA Oil Tanker Fleet

Majority stake in a company owning and managing Aframax oil tankers. Operations spanning technical and commercial management with offices in the US and Greece, trading vessels on the spot charter market.

Real Estate & Mixed-Use Development

The Rhodes Marina €600M GDV, Greece

Largest mixed-use waterfront development in Greece. Master plan by Benoy, permitted for 90,000 m² with 572+ marina berths, commercial space, and super yacht accommodations. Over €60M already invested in construction and infrastructure.

Real Estate & Historic Redevelopment

Cincinnati Terrace Plaza 600,000 sq ft Mixed-Use

Iconic 24-story Modernist building designed by Skidmore, Owings & Merrill (1948). Acquired at foreclosure auction. Under redevelopment with a $75M public-private partnership including 162 luxury residential units, a 262-room hotel reflag, and retail renovation.

This proven foundation of cross-sector investing, global partnerships, and hands-on value creation is what powers Platinum Venture Labs as it enters the next frontier.
